Welcome!

I'm an aspiring front-end web developer with a background in business-to-business print design and publishing. While I focus on changing my career, I'm working part time as the production editor of a twice-monthly magazine where I'm responsible for taking Word documents and turning them into a finished magazine - and for being the resident grammer obsessive.

I love good design and how it can make life better, easier and more beautiful, and truly believe there's no excuse for ugly site layout - which is why I decided to pursue front-end web development. I'm also fascinated by why people make the choices they do and can't wait to start delving deeper into the world of UI.

Your accent is a bit...

I'm Anglo-American; I was born near Seattle, Washington in the US and moved to the UK nearly 14 years ago for uni, where I finished with a BA (Hons) Documentary Photography. Which, as you can see, has led to a successful career in that artistic field!
